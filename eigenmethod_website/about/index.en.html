About the company

EigenMethod is a team of 50 high-qualified developers. Our goal is to help our customers to grow their businesses using the latest software technologies and appropriate development methodologies.

We are good at Open Source technology stack: Python, C++, PostgreSQL, Django, Twisted, Tornado, Redis and others.

We are experienced in development of applications, which require deep integration into SAP systems. We provide services for outsourcing of development in SAP HANA, SAPUI5 and ABAP.

We have also created some of own products like our mobile platform.

Our offices are located in Eastern Europe, which is a kind of pool of IT experts. We are a high-skilled team, able to offer our customers cost-effective solutions and services in information technologies.
