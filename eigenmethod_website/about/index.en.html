About the company

EigenMethod is a young team of over 50 high-skilled developers. We develop our own software products as well as assist other companies in successful implementation of their projects in software development.

We use the most advanced information technologies and development methodologies to develop our products. The software products are developed with open technology stack: Python, C++, PostgreSQL, Django, Twisted, Tornado, Redis etc.

We are experienced in development of applications, which require deep integration into SAP systems. We provide services for outsourcing of development in SAP HANA, SAPUI5 and ABAP.

Our offices are located in Eastern Europe, which is a kind of pool of IT experts. We are a high-skilled team, able to offer our customers cost-effective solutions for development of own products.
