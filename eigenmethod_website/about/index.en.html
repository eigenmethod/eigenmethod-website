About the company

EigenMethod is a team of 50 high-qualified developers. Our goal is to help our customers to grow their businesses using the latest software technologies and appropriate development methodologies.

We are good at Open Source technology stack: Python, C++, PostgreSQL, Django, Twisted, Tornado, Redis and others.

We are creating also own software applications and products.

We have an experience in development of applications with SAP system integration. We provide also services of outsourcing development on SAP HANA, SAPUI5 and ABAP.

Our offices are in the Eastern Europe which are rich in smart and efficient IT specialists. We can bring the best combination of highly skilled but cost effective team and provide the best cost/benefit ratio.
