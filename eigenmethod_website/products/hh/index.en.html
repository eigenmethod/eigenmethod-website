What is HyperHive?

HyperHive is an innovative product in the market of Mobile Application Development Platform (MADP) solutions, which renders it possible to developers and companies to promptly develop and implement mobile solutions due to availability of a wide range of ready templates for solution of sample tasks, brought together into a single platform.

The mobile platform HyperHive is a turnkey technology stack of ready scenarios and templates for execution of sample mobile development tasks.

The platform is integrated into the solution architecture in two simple steps:

Connection of various data sources to the user-friendly interface with the least steps and parameters

Connection of HyperHive framework to the mobile application project
